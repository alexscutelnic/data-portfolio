<h1>Sales & Margin Power BI Report</h1>

This folder contains an anonymised PDF export of a Power BI dashboard used for month-end reporting. All client, supplier, and personnel names have been fully anonymised.

<hr>

<h2>Overview</h2>

The report provides a complete view of business performance, focusing on:
Actual Sales vs Budget
Actual Sales vs Last Year
Gross Margin vs Budget
Gross Margin vs Last Year
Sales and Margin trends by Month
Top Clients by Total Sales
Sales Person performance comparison
Drill-through to detailed pages

All sensitive information has been fully anonymised through:

Removal of metadata
